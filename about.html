Our Story

Hours of Operation:

  • Monday-Thursday: 10:00am-9:00pm

Pizza Planet began in 1973 when two college roommates wanted to take the pizza universe by geomagnetic storm. Amdromeda Skye and Galileo Mars wanted to create a family-friendly celebration of all things cheesy and delicious. The restaurant opened up in Orlando, Florida on an unusually cold day. However, Venus was in retrograde, so the anomaly should have been foreseeable.

In addition to a full size dining room, Pizza Planet boast a massive arcade cave with over 42 far out adventures to conquer. Our games aren’t only for the kids! In fact, our top score on “Alien Operation” is held by Travis W., a father from Richmond, VA.

Bring by the whole family today and blast off to an exciting night. We also host large and small events including:

  • birthday parties
  • launch parties
  • wedding receptions
  • and more
